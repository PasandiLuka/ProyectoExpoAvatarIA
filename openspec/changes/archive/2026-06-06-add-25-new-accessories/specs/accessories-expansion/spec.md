## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: New hair styles
The system SHALL provide 5 additional hair styles (styles 10-14) rendered via CSS with visually distinct silhouettes and colors.

#### Scenario: Each style is visually unique
- **WHEN** user cycles through hair styles 10-14
- **THEN** each style shows a clearly different silhouette (mohawk, braids, curls, fade, japanese)

### Requirement: New shirts including meme and country designs
The system SHALL provide 6 new shirt designs: shirt-67 (number 67 + "sixseven"), Mexico (green/white/red with Zapdos eagle), USA (stars and stripes), Canada (maple leaf), South Korea (taeguk), England (St. George's cross).

#### Scenario: Shirt 67 shows meme design
- **WHEN** user selects "67" shirt
- **THEN** a large "67" is displayed with "sixseven" text below it on a dark background

#### Scenario: Mexico shirt uses Zapdos as eagle
- **WHEN** user selects "México" shirt
- **THEN** the Zapdos PNG is displayed centered on green/white/red tricolor stripes

### Requirement: New hats
The system SHALL provide 5 new hats: witch (SVG pointed), top hat (CSS), beanie (CSS), pirate tricorn (SVG), cat ears (SVG).

### Requirement: New glasses
The system SHALL provide 5 new glasses: round Lennon-style (CSS), heart-shaped (SVG), star-shaped (SVG), cybernetic scanner (SVG), diva extravagant (CSS).

### Requirement: New hand items
The system SHALL provide 5 new hand items: drum (SVG), cane (SVG), olympic torch (SVG), shield (CSS), flower bouquet (CSS).
